Welcome to our school!

We offer a large outdoor area including different playgrounds for both younger and older children. We know that children learn best when they get fresh air and movement, so we prioritize getting them active and outdoors.

We offer care for infants 6 weeks of age up to school-aged children. We pride ourselves on being family-run, and ensure your child feels right at home.

Children are abe to work collaboratively to improve school-readiness skills as well as social and emotional learning. We build in plenty of time for them to be creative through a variety of art activities.
