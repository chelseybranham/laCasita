Our Story

La Casita was founded by Consuelo Aramburo. Consuelo (Ms. Connie) has been in childcare for over 20 years. She first started in the home setting as a licensed Department of Health and Safety Childcare provider. It has always been Connie's dream to open up her own center, and that is exactly what she did! La Casita Preschool Childcare opened in May of 2021 and is now coming up on 2 years of business. Connie and her family have loved this experience and the bond they have built with the children and families over the last two years. La Casita is currently looking to expand and make an impact on families in even more communities!
